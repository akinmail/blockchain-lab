INSIDE AGENCEME

AN INTERNATIONAL
AGENCY

AgenceMe was founded in 2011 by Marine LeGrand and Barthelemy Chalvet. In 2012 Barthelemy was offered the position of Lead Designer by Xola, a San Francisco based startup. It was through this experience that we formed the operational direction that guides the way we work with our clients to this day. We moved to Silicon Valley to work with Xola for 3 months. This first experience abroad allowed us to enrich both our technical and creative skills while also establishing a tight-knit relationship with our customer by working with them in close proximity. We not only found that we were able to gain a deep understanding of their universe, but we also learned valuable workplace techniques that helped optimize the way that we get things done. Such as organizing our work into "sprints", a practice commonly adopted by companies in San Francisco. Upon returning to France, we decided to pursue a similar dynamic with each of our customers by offering to “move in with them” for short periods of time. Working so closely with our customers to better understanding their activities, needs, targets, and objectives has become our main motivation.

THE AGENCY INSITE
LE STUDIO COWORKING

VISIT WEBSITE

Our own coworking space in France and set up our agency there. Thus, Studio Coworking was born in June 2016.

During our travels we have had to, on many occasions and to this day, search for the best workplace in a given city. Top criteria include high speed wifi and good coffee. This is how we discovered places like WeWork in New York, Le Fab Cafe in Tokyo and Halte 24-7 in Montreal. We enjoyed these spaces not just for their optics, but also for the relationships thatthey can help create in giving freelancers and startups a chance to meet. That’s why we decided to create our own coworking space in Rouen and set up our agency there. Thus, Studio Coworking was born in June 2016.
